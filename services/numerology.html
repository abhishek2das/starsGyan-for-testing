Numerology

Numerology is the predictive science of the influence of numbers in our life. Each number has inherent vibration similar to a specific planet. Numbers 1 to 9 governs by Sun, Moon, Jupiter, Rahu, Mercury, Venus, Ketu, Saturn, and Mars respectively. Based on the numerological analysis, a numerologist can determine a person’s lucky day, lucky or friendly number, lucky name, lucky year, and many more things. Root number or Driver number or Mulank is a single number obtained by adding the birth date of a person. For example, a person’s date of birth is 23 July then his root number will be 2+3= 5. Every root number has specific traits like:

Born on 1, 10, 19, or 28 of any month are inventive, leader, determined, ambitious, authoritative.

Born on 2, 11, 20, or 29 of any month are romantic, imaginative, artistic, lack of continuity in plans.

Born on 3,12, 21, or 30 of any month are decidedly ambitious, dissatisfied in a subordinate position, learned, excellent in execution of commands, discipline, good financial knowledgeable.

Born on 4, 13, 22, or 31 of any month are technology lover, rebellious, has an unconventional view of social reform, feel lonely and isolated.

Born on 5, 14, or 23of any month are versatile, talkative, friendly, hyperactive, impulsive, resentful but pleased easily.

Born on 6, 15, or 24 of any month are blessed, fun-loving, charismatic, friendly, popular, materialistic, tactful, luxurious.

Born on 7, 16, or 25 of any month are straightforward, restless mind, mysterious, spiritual, philosophical, has the power of independent thinking.

Born on 8, 17, or 26 of any month are practical, deep and intense nature, revolutionary, feel lonely at heart, face struggles, life is full of ups and downs, like a solid foundation in everything.

Born on 9, 18, or 27 of any month are fighters, strong will power, impulsive, resourceful, surprise the world with their deeds, aggressive, adventures.

Name correction is a very useful tool in numerology in which according to a person’s lucky number, a numerologist proposes some changes in the name of the person or institution, to make it lucky for that person. There are many examples available worldwide where big celebrities and companies changed their name by adding extra alphabets to get remarkable success.
